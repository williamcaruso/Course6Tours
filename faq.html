Frequently Asked Questions


What is Course 6?
At MIT everything is a number! Course 6 (six) refers to the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.


When should I schedule a tour?
It is usually typical to schedule tours around a week or two in advance. This gives the tourguides time to check busy schedules and confirm they can take you.


What is a Course 6 Tour?
A Course 6 Tour is a publicly available tour of the EECS department at MIT for prospective undergraduate and graduate students


How can I schedule Course 6 Tour?

Schedule a Tour

If you have any problems with scheduling, please contact Anne Hunter at anneh@mit.edu


Are tours handicap accessible?
Yes, Course 6 Tours are handicap accessible. There are both stair and elevator options throughout the tour and are restrooms along the route.


What should I bring?
Questions! We love your questions. The best experiences are those when visitors know the school and want to find out more about how daily life in Course 6 is like! Also all tours are indoors but be aware that Boston is known to be pretty cold during the winter.
